## 📖 **What's in the User Guide**

### **🚀 Getting Started (2 Minutes)**
- Register account
- Browse 51 Thai words
- Listen to pronunciation

### **📚 Main Features**
1. **Dashboard** - Progress stats and CEFR breakdown
2. **Browse** - Explore vocabulary with filters
3. **Learn** - Study new words with examples
4. **Review** - Flashcard-style spaced repetition
5. **Driving Mode** 🚗 - Hands-free audio learning

### **🚗 Driving Mode (NEW!)**
Three learning modes:
- **Random Words** - No login required, explore vocabulary
- **Recently Learned** - Review your recent words
- **Due for Review** - Practice words needing review

Settings:
- Speed: 0.7x - 1.2x
- Repeat word at end
- Include examples
- Pause duration

### **🧠 Spaced Repetition**
Smart scheduling with SM-2 algorithm:
- Day 0: Learn word
- Day 1: First review → Next: 3 days
- Day 4: Second review → Next: 7 days
- Day 11: Third review → Next: 15 days
- Day 26: Mastered! ⭐

### **🎯 Daily Learning Flow**
- **Morning (5 min)**: Review due items
- **Commute (10 min)**: Driving Mode
- **Evening (5 min)**: Learn 3-5 new words

### **🎉 30-Day Challenge**
- Week 1: Learn 10 A1 words
- Week 2: Learn 15 A1-A2 words + Start Driving Mode
- Week 3: Learn 20 A2-B1 words + Track progress
- Week 4: Master 50+ words + Conversational basics!

### **🔑 Password Reset (v1.6.0)**
- Click "Forgot Password"
- Enter email
- Check inbox for reset link
- Create new password
- Security: 1-hour token expiry
